Account & Billing FAQs

To reset your password:

  1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
  2. Enter your registered email address
  3. Check your email for the reset link (check spam folder too)
  4. Click the link and create a new strong password
  5. Log in with your new credentials

If you don't receive the email within 5 minutes, contact our support team.

To update your payment method:

  1. Go to your Dashboard
  2. Click "Billing & Subscription" in the sidebar
  3. Select "Payment Methods"
  4. Click "Add New Payment Method"
  5. Enter your card details and save
  6. You can set it as default or remove old methods

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period. To cancel:

  1. Navigate to "Billing & Subscription" in your Dashboard
  2. Click on your active plan
  3. Select "Cancel Subscription"
  4. Follow the prompts to confirm

You'll receive a confirmation email once cancelled. You can resubscribe anytime.

Troubleshooting FAQs

If you're having trouble logging in, try these steps:

  • Check your credentials: Ensure you're using the correct email and password (case-sensitive)
  • Reset password: Use the "Forgot Password" option if you've forgotten it
  • Clear browser cache: Sometimes cached data causes login issues
  • Try different browser: Switch to Chrome, Firefox, or Edge
  • Disable extensions: Some browser extensions may interfere
  • Check internet connection: Ensure you have stable internet

If problems persist, contact support with details about the error message you're seeing.

Video playback issues can be caused by several factors:

  • Internet speed: Ensure you have at least 5 Mbps for HD streaming
  • Browser compatibility: Use the latest version of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge
  • Clear cache: Clear your browser's cache and cookies
  • Disable ad blockers: Some ad blockers interfere with video players
  • Update browser: Make sure your browser is up to date
  • Check firewall: Ensure your firewall isn't blocking video content

You can also try switching video quality to a lower setting in the player controls.

Course Enrollment FAQs

Enrolling in a course is simple:

  1. Browse the Course Catalog
  2. Click on a course that interests you
  3. Click the "Enroll Now" button on the course page
  4. If the course is free, you'll get immediate access
  5. If it's a paid course, complete the checkout process
  6. Once enrolled, the course appears in your Dashboard

Some courses may have prerequisites that need to be completed first.

  • Mobile browsers: Access through Chrome, Safari, or Firefox on your phone or tablet
  • App experience: Add SkillSync to your home screen for an app-like experience
  • Features available: Watch videos, take quizzes, access materials, track progress
